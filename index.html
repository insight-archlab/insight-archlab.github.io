About

I am an assistant professor of computer science at The College of William and Mary (W&M) (US News Rankings) in Virginia, USA. At W&M, I am leading Insight Computer Architecture Lab with the goal to generate insights that would enable more energy-efficient, reliable, secure, capable, and useful computing systems

I am currently teaching: CSCI 680: Parallel Computer Architecture
