Insight Computer Architecture Lab @ William and Mary

The high-level goal of the lab is to generate novel insights, which would faciliate in building future computing systems more energy-efficient, reliable, secure, capable, and useful.

We are looking for self-motivated students to work with. Current topics of interest include (but not limited to): GPU Architectures, Approximate Computing, Hardware Security and Reliability, and Non-traditional Architectures (Automata Processor, Quantum, DNA etc.). Email Prof. Adwait Jog with your CV, if you are interested.
