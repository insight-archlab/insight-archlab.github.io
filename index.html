Welcome to Insight Architecture Lab @ College of William and Mary

The high-level goal of the lab is to generate novel insights, which would faciliate in building future computing systems more energy-efficient, reliable, secure, capable, and useful.

The lab is looking for self-motivated students. Multiple funded positions are available. If you are interested in doing research in the general area of computer architecture, please email Prof. Adwait Jog with your CV. We are especially looking for students who are interested in the areas of GPU computing, memory systems, and emerging/non-traditional computing hardware (e.g., quantum and DNA) architectures. Students interested in other disciplines such as (but not limited to) biology, economics, machine learning, physics, security, are also welcome to apply.
